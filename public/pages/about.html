About Me

Francis Githua

Computer Science Student · Network Engineer · Security Enthusiast

Hey! I'm Francis — a Computer Science student at Kirinyaga University, Kenya, focusing on Networking and Cybersecurity. I started CyberNotes to document my learning journey and share practical knowledge about network engineering and ethical hacking.

I'm currently pursuing my CCNA certification and actively building skills through hands-on practice — setting up GNS3 labs, experimenting with pfSense firewalls, and hacking machines on TryHackMe.

My philosophy: the best way to understand security is to think like an attacker — ethically and responsibly. Everything on this blog is for educational purposes, aimed at helping students and professionals level up their skills.
